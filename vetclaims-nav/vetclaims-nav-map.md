## Page inventory + descriptions

### Conversion path (the money funnel)
- **Home** — flat-fee VA claim prep, "VA rating you deserve?"; 4.87★ (207), 43K+ vets, file-ready in 14 days.
- **See if I Qualify (opt-in)** — `optin.vetclaims.ai`, "Your rating increases… or We Pay You", Purple Heart Foundation endorsement, SMS opt-in baked in. Quiz → contact form → **submit = real sales lead** (gated; not submitted).
- **Pricing** — $1,250 flat "Complete Care", explicitly **no % of backpay** (their wedge vs attorneys).
- **Client Login** — `vetclaims.app` portal for existing paying clients.

### VA Benefits (educational hub feeding the funnel)

- **Rating Increase** · **VA Disability Pay** · **Appeals** (Supplemental/HLR/Board) · **C&P Exams** · **PACT Act & Toxic Exposure**. Each sub-hub anchors 5–12 long-tail SEO articles.

### Conditions
- **Conditions hub** — "VA Disability Ratings by Condition"; links to 30+ condition pages (PTSD, tinnitus, sleep apnea, GERD, back pain, ~10 arthritis variants…).

### Company
- **About** — veteran-led, AI-powered claim prep. · **Founder Story** — Lukas Simianer, Purple Heart vet. · **Contact** — (737) 344-4399.

### Tools ⭐ (their free top-of-funnel — most relevant to Berry Law's decision)
- **VA Disability Calculator** (`calc.vetclaims.ai`) — interactive combined-rating + monthly-pay calculator. Pick body part (bilateral factor) + rating %. Captured **in action**: 50% + 30% + 20% → **combined 70% · $1,759.19/mo**.
- **Patriots Portal** — "Skip the BS, get the rating you deserve" (browser-extension / portal play).
- **Free Nexus Templates** — nexus-letter examples + downloadable templates (lead magnet).

### Blog
- **Blog index** — 21 posts on claims, rating increases, C&P, secondaries, appeals.

## Competitive read (for the "build a free tool" decision)
- VetClaims runs the exact playbook Berry Law is weighing: **free tools (calculator, Patriots Portal extension, nexus templates) + a ~145-page SEO content engine** → funnels into a **$1,250 flat-fee human service**.
- The **calculator is the strongest free hook** — instant, useful, captures intent, and hands off to "Book Free Strategy Call".
- Differentiator they hammer everywhere: **flat fee, no cut of your backpay** (vs attorneys' %).
- vs **VeteranAI** (the other teardown): VeteranAI = free self-serve AI SaaS ($25/mo Pro); VetClaims = lead-gen funnel to a done-for-you human service. Same audience, opposite delivery.
